Overview

Site Name

Sankofa Chamber of Commerce

The name is taken from the popular ghanaian adinkra symbols. Being a community dominated by ghanaians and other africans, the name unifies the people culturally. Additionally, the name teaches the wisdom of learning from the past to build for the future.

Purpose

The Sankofa chamber of commerce serves as a representative for businesses in the adenta community. The organization helps promote businesses the organization of fairs and conferences. In addition, we serve as an advocate for small scale businesses in the area.

Audience

All large or small business owners and potential business owners within the community.
