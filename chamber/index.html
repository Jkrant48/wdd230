Sankofa Chamber of Commerce

We are an organisation consisting of members committed to providing business and job opportunities so that people can find work and provide for their families.

We connect to inspire, empower and educate.

At Sankofa, we believe in the transformative power of networking and collaboration. Through our vibrant community of businesses, we foster an environment that encourages innovation and growth. Whether you are a seasoned entrepreneur or just starting your journey, our chamber is your gateway to meaningful connections that inspire creativity, empower your business endeavors, and provide invaluable educational resources. Join us in building a thriving network that transcends boundaries and propels businesses toward success. Together, we connect, inspire, empower, and educate; forging a future of prosperity for all.
